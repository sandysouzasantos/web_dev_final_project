"Before healing others, heal yourself."

Welcome! Balanced Life is a website and newsletter dedicated to bringing together contents about physical, emotional and mental health for a balanced life.

We know that for a well being these three spheres are equally important and must be in harmony. So with this intent, our weekly updates will give you more insights into these matters to inspire you in the search of what fits for you and thus, be better for yourself and others.

Living in a more harmonious way, having strength, balance and wisdom will not only change you, but the world around too.
