Physical Health

"Keeping your body healthy is an expression of gratitude to the whole cosmos – the trees, the clouds, everything."
— Thich Nhat Hanh

It's time to move your body!

It is common sense that we must keep moving our bodies. But it should be neither about calories nor unreachable beauty standards.

When we putting some effort into something our main goals must be our health and personal satisfaction, including physical activities.

Exercising nowadays it is not a natural thing, you need to construct the habit. But before registering yourself at gym, you should be aware of what type of activity fits better for you and gives you pleasure to keep going.
